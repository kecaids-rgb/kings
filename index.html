💡 Innovation in Monetisation

Our Top 7 Passive Income Streams

Why rely on one income source when students have so many pockets? Here's how we diversify our portfolio.

📄
01

The Bona-fide Certificate Hustle

You need a certificate to prove you're a student of the college that is already charging you lakhs. Totally makes sense, right? We call it "administrative overhead." You can call it whatever you want — just pay up first.

Use cases: Bank loan, scholarship, hostel, bus pass, basically breathing.

₹150–₹300 per certificate 🤑
💼
02

Placement Training (After Charging Lakhs)

We already charged you ₹1.5 Lakhs a year in tuition. But actual job preparation? That's a premium add-on, bestie. For just ₹5,000–₹15,000 extra, we'll teach you how to make a resume. (Word template included. Maybe.)

Results not guaranteed. Trainer may or may not have ever worked in industry. Vibes-based learning only.

₹5,000–₹15,000 extra 😤
📓
03

The Sacred Exam Booklet Tax

You bought the pen. You paid the exam fee. You crammed all night. But the blank pages to write your answers on? Oh no no no, that's a separate charge. We call it "stationery management." You'll call it something less publishable.

Pro tip: Never ask for receipts. The bursar's office will develop amnesia.

₹30–₹100 per booklet 📚
📚
04

Pay Book Fee Now, Get Books Never (or After Exams)

We collect the book fee at the start of the semester — upfront, non-refundable, fully serious. The books themselves? They'll arrive after your exams are over. Think of it as a mystery box. The mystery is why you're paying for it.

Useful for: decoration, doorstop, evidence. Not useful for: studying.

Paid upfront. Delivered post-exam. 📦
🚌
05

Industrial Visit: Collect Money, Cancel Trip

Step 1: Announce an exciting industrial visit to boost morale. Step 2: Collect ₹800–₹1,500 per student for "logistics and coordination." Step 3: Cancel the visit. Reason: "unforeseen circumstances." Step 4: Keep the money. Logistics are expensive, after all.

Rescheduled date: TBD. Refund date: Never.

₹800–₹1,500 vanished into thin air 🌫️
⏰
06

The 1-Hour Late Fine Strategy

Fee due date: 15th of the month. You paid on the 16th at 9 AM. Congratulations — you are now subject to a late fine. One hour. One day. One week. Doesn't matter, the fine is the same. We believe punctuality is a virtue worth monetising.

Sunday falling on due date? Still your problem. Bank holiday? Still your problem.

₹200–₹500 fine for being human 🕐
🗒️
07

The Premium Record Note (A.K.A. Stapled A4 Sheets)

For lab practicals, you must buy our official record note — which is literally A4 sheets so thin you can read through them, bound together with the structural integrity of wet tissue paper. Available exclusively from us. At a price that implies it's hand-crafted leather.

You could buy better from a roadside stall for ₹10. But that's not "official."

₹50–₹80 for glorified A4 📄

The Official College Rules

Printed in the handbook. Enforced selectively. Questioned never.

⚠️ TOP PRIORITY
★

🎄 Holidays: Only Christmas is Respected

Diwali? Classes. Pongal? Classes. Eid? Classes. Onam? Half-day if you're lucky. But Christmas? Full holiday, no questions asked. We don't know why either. It has always been this way. Complaining about this will be noted in your attendance file.

01

🚫 No Laptops in the CS Department

This is a Computer Science branch. We have a computer lab for that. The lab has computers that work almost never, are running Windows XP, and have keyboards missing at least 3 keys. But they are official computers. Your personal, working, fully charged laptop is a distraction. Leave it home.

02

📅 10-Day "Summer Break" to Complete Your 1-Month Internship

After semester exams, you get exactly 10 days before the next semester starts. Your university requires a 1-month internship. We see no conflict here. Figure it out. No On-Duty (OD) will be granted for internship days. If you want OD — get a bona-fide certificate (₹200) and beg the HOD. HOD is on leave. Come next week.

03

🗒️ Buy Your "Official" Record Note From Us

For your lab practicals, you must maintain a record note — which we sell exclusively. It is A4 sheets, stapled on one side, thin enough to see through, with a printed cover that says "Record Note." The structural integrity is comparable to a wet napkin. Price: definitely more than it costs us to make. Alternative sources: not permitted. "But sir, this is the same as—" — Shut up and buy it.

04

📚 Library Hours (Terms & Conditions Apply)

The library is open! However, you may only visit during your free periods. You have no free periods. There is also no dedicated library period in the timetable. The library exists. Whether you can ever access it is a philosophical question we encourage you to resolve on your own time — which you also don't have.

05

📞 Complaints? Call Our Helpline™

We have a 24/7 student helpline for all grievances. It responds promptly — if you are a new admission enquiry. Existing student problems (fee issues, harassment, missing books, cancelled IV) will be addressed in the order received, which is never. Please hold. Your suffering is important to us.
